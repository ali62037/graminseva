About Gramin Seva

An online platform called Digital Gramin Seva has been established to help anyone who wish to establish Grahak Seva Kendras in their communities. Seeking to make India a Knowledge economy and society enabled by technology, the portal is a component of the Digital India programme

The only platform in India that enables anybody to create a Grahak Seva Kendra without requiring a qualification or test is Digital Gramin Seva. People, particularly those living in rural regions, now find it simpler to launch their own companies and offer their own companies and offer their communities much-needed services.

Users may navigate and finish the application process with ease because to the portal's user-friendly interface. Basic details about the candidate, including their educational background, intended services for their Grahak Seva Kendras, and personal information, are required during the application process. Following submission, the application is examined by the authorities, and the applicant receives an email notifying them if it is accepted.

Grahak Seva Kendras are playing a crucial role in bridging the digital divide and enabling citizens in rural areas to access services that were previously unavailable to them. By providing a range of services through their Grahak Seva Kendra, including banking, insurance, utility bill payments, digital literacy, education, and e-governance services, people are able to create a digitally inclusive community.
